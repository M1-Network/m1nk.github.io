M1 Network

M1 network is a decentralized cloud rendering platform for gamefi, virtual live broadcasts, and panoramic social scenes. It naturally supports the data design of the blockchain, and will be the infrastructure for the high-definition, full-realistic visualization of the metaverse in the WEB3 era. M1 Network is a project that helps every chain and every user quickly realize and experience the high-definition full-realistic visualization metaverse service. It is committed to providing each chain with a high-definition, full-realistic metaverse ecology, and helping each chain to quickly form its own cosmic civilization. We also provide metaverse pass-through service for every user. We realize the rendering and use of high-definition full-realistic visualization of each metaverse service through the edge cloud rendering container. Metaverse players can switch services among different metaverses on the M1 Network through their own coins.
